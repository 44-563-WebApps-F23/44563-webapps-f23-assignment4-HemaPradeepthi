Hema Pradeepthi acts in a play

The Master Builder Act 2 (Henrik Ibsen)

KAIA.

Then take a little walk.



RAGNAR.

Yes, do. I will come with you.



BROVIK.

[With warmth.]

I will not go till he comes! I and determined to have it out this evening with--

[in a tone of suppressed bitterness]

--with him--with the chief.



KAIA.

[Anxiously.]

Oh no, uncle,--do wait awhile before doing that!



RAGNAR.

Yes, better wait, father!



BROVIK.

[Draws is breath laboriously.]

Ha--ha--! _I_ haven't much time for waiting.



KAIA

[Listening.]

Hush! I hear him on the stairs.

[All three go back to their work. A short silence. HALVARD SOLNESS comes in through the hall door. He is a man no longer young, but healthy and vigorous, with close-cut curly hair, dark moustache and dark thick eyebrows. He wears a greyish-green buttoned jacket with an upstanding collar and broad lapels. On his head he wears a soft grey felt hat, and he has one or two light portfolios under his arm.]
